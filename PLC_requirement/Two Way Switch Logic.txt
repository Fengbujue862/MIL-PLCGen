Write a self-contained 61131-3 structured text program, not a function block, to implement a Two Way Switch Logic. In duplex type house there are ground floor and first floor. Sometimes people need to go from ground floor to first floor or from first floor to ground floor by staircase provided in house. a lamp/light is use to see the steps of the staircase. using PLC to control this lamp using two switches, one switch at ground floor and second switch at first floor to control one lamp. the users can switch ON/OFF the light whether they are on bottom of the stair or at top.
约束：点击任意按钮都改变电灯开关状态
